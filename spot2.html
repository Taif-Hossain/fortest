Cox’s Bazar

Cox’s Bazar is one of the most popular tourist spot in Bangladesh which is situated in the most southern part and the largest
(112 km) sea beach in the world. It is the best place to enjoy the scene of sunrise and sunset.The cool fresh air and peaceful weather on the shore will surely make you relaxed. So for a serene and relaxing vacation, this place is mostly recommended to the visitors. (more...)
